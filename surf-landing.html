The Experience

What to expect on the day

No experience necessary. Here's how a typical lesson unfolds from arrival to your first ride.

01

Arrive and Gear Up

Meet your instructor, collect your board and wetsuit, and settle in with a welcome briefing.

02

Beach Theory

Learn the pop-up technique, paddling mechanics, and essential ocean etiquette — all on dry sand.

03

Into the Water

Wade in with your instructor by your side. Practice paddling and positioning in controlled shorebreak.

04

Catch Waves

Your instructor picks the wave, you paddle, pop up, and ride. Most students stand by session one.

05

Review and Celebrate

Back on the beach for video playback, feedback, and a well-earned cold drink.

Safety and Trust

We take the ocean seriously so you don't have to worry

The ocean is powerful and we respect it. Every precaution is taken so your only job is to have fun.

  • ✓Trained water lifeguard present at every session
  • ✓Beginner sessions held in supervised, beginner-friendly breaks
  • ✓Rip current awareness taught before entering water
  • ✓Soft foam boards used for all beginner and kids lessons
  • ✓Lessons rescheduled if conditions are unsafe
🌊

Ocean Reading

We brief you on wave selection, rip currents, and safe entry/exit before every session.

🏥

First Aid Ready

All instructors hold current first aid certification. A kit is always on the beach.

☀️

Sun and Heat Management

We time sessions to avoid peak sun, provide rash guards, and remind you to hydrate.

📡

Daily Surf Check

Instructors review forecasts each morning to select the safest break for the day.
